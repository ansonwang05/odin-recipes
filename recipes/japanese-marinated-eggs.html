Japanese Soy-Cured Ramen Eggs

“A bowl of ramen with an egg and some sort of sushi. Food noodle ramen.”, by Pixabay

Description

Japanese ramen eggs, or ajitsuke tamago, are the perfect balance of flavour and texture - rich, soft-boiled yolk enveloped in a savoury soy marinade.

They‘re a signature addition to ramen bowls, but did you know they can also be a snack or a topping for rice dishes?

Traditionally served at ramen shops, these eggs are marinated to perfection, taking only a few hours to transform from simple eggs into flavour bombs. They‘re the little touch that turns a good bowl of ramen into something unforgettable.
